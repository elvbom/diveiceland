Dive Iceland - Your guide to diving in Iceland

Our Dive Tours

We at DiveIceland.com organize dive tours all over Iceland. Please take a look at some tours we have ready for you. Many different tours can be organized, you tell us what you want and we can make a tour regarding to your wishes. Please contact us on (+354) 699 3000 or info@diveiceland.com for more information.

  • Dive the Kleifarvatn thermal area!
    We now offer a tour to Kleifarvatn geothermal area. We will make your dive a once in a lifetime experience.
  • Dive Silfran!
    For a diver coming to Iceland there is one must do dive; Silfra! A fresh water site which has been rated as one of the world's top three dive sites!
  • Dive the Atlanic Ocean!

Why not get your PADI Dry Suit certification? Do your dry suit course in combination with any day tour. Contact us for more details.

Meet The Team

This is not the best web site you find about diving, but we dont think there is one, where you find more information about diving in Iceland.
The reason our web site is not the best is because we are diving professionals, not computer experts. We do have 15 years experience doing diving tours, so you can book your once in a lifetime experience diving in Iceland, with us, with confidence.

Hedinn is our head Instructor with over 10 years experience in doing courses and guided tours, we consider us lucky to have him working with us.

We at DiveIceland.com are doing all PADI courses from Open Water up to Divemaster, selling diving equipment and doing shorter and longer tours for divers from all over the world. So for you travelling to Iceland, or you staying in Iceland for a shorter or a longer time, check out our courses and/or tours.

DiveIceland located in Hafnarfjordur (about 10 km from Reykjavik city center). If you wish to go diving please contact us on (+354) 699 3000 or by sending us a email. We will pick you up at your Reykjavik location.

We offer all diving courses in English as well as Icelandic. Send us email at info@diveiceland.com to ask for when the next course for English speaking will be done.
